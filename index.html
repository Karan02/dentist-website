Our Philosophy

The preservation of your teeth is our top priority.

If damage to your teeth has already been caused by tooth decay or a disease of the periodontium, it is important to me to provide you with a replacement that corresponds aesthetically to your ideal and is functionally oriented to your natural tooth.

I am happy to advise you on my ordination and take the time to explain to you the advantages and disadvantages of the different forms of treatment.

Dear patient!The dental practice Lach is a unique, integrated dental center in Lower Austria, which includes the dental treatment, the dental technician and a laboratory in a house.

Through this close cooperation and integration, we can assure you of the best quality and the fastest possible implementation. The aesthetic result can also be optimized by the proximity to the dental technician: For example, a dental crown can be adapted exactly to the color of your natural teeth. Prosthesis repairs can be performed on the same day of your visit . Nevertheless, for us the highest possible long and healthy preservation of your natural teeth. We would be glad if you entrust us!
